MOMO

One of my favourtie food is Momo from my home country Neapl. Nepal is a land of vibrant culture and breathtaking landscapes, but it is also a food lover’s paradise. One of the most popular dishes in Nepal is momos, which are traditional Nepalese dumplings that are steamed or fried and filled with a variety of meat or vegetables. Momos are a staple food in Nepal and can be found in almost every corner of the country, from small street vendors to fancy restaurants.
